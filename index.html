martina valente (1998, Venice IT) is a graphic, editorial, and visual designer. Her work moves across different type of media –photography, magazines, video and motion design– approached as spaces for visual and design research.

    She is interested in contemporary visual art scene and in discovering the work of independent artists. In recent years, she has developed a strong interest in art direction, particularly through collaborations with professionals from fields beyond graphic design. In her projects, she values the layering of languages and meanings.

    She is currently part of the collective sweetdreams studio, together with Noa Merlini and Agnese Pozzobon, where she continues to deepen her skills and expand her practice. ♥
